Did you know that recent studies have shown that approximately 70% of the female population is financially illiterate, making it less likely for women to invest, compounding any financial disadvantages and limiting their collective influence globally. Hejfin intents to make this situation better.

Our goal

Our goal is to provide a one-stop reliable financial education app for female/non-binary people to help them improve the management of their personal finances as well as start investing. The users will get reliable and jargon-free information from the platform which aims to provide knowledge, skills, and fostering the motivation to engage with responsible investing.
